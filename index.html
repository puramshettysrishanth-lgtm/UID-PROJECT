About MRF Tyres

MRF (Madras Rubber Factory) is India's largest tyre manufacturer, founded in 1946 and headquartered in Chennai. Starting as a toy balloon manufacturer, MRF grew into a global tyre powerhouse.

Today, MRF exports to over 90 countries and manufactures tyres for every segment — cars, bikes, trucks, tractors and more. With world-class R&D and a passion for motorsports, MRF is synonymous with trust on Indian roads.
